About Us

Welcome to Bella Ciao Restaurant, a culinary gem located in the heart of Peshawar. At Bella Ciao, we believe in creating unforgettable dining experiences by blending authentic flavors with a modern twist. Our menu is a celebration of Italian and continental cuisine, offering everything from mouth-watering pizzas and pastas to delectable desserts and refreshing drinks. Whether you're here for a casual meal or a special occasion, our warm ambiance and attentive service will make you feel right at home. Join us at Bella Ciao, where every meal is a celebration of taste, tradition, and togetherness. Bella Ciao is a family-owned restaurant that brings the flavors of Italy to your table in Peshawar.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Bella Ciao was born from a passion for Italian cuisine. Our founder, having spent years in Italy, brought back the recipes, traditions, and warmth of Italian hospitality to Peshawar.
